Membership

CATEGORIES OF MEMBERSHIP

Know more about our membership

Full Membership

This will be open to anyone who is a registered pharmacist, a postgraduate student,
graduate demonstrator or an holder of a higher degree in pharmacognosy
and those involved in natural product research as evidenced by publications, whether he or she is in teaching, research, public or private sector.

Associate Membership

This will be open to other interested persons as shall be prescribed by regulations from time to time.

Become a Member of NSP

We create opportunities that benefit our members.
We support and fund thriving projects that will help in the development of Pharmacognosy in Nigeria.
