We are a fast-growing community of drug research scientists

Welcome to the Nigerian Society of Pharmacognosy (NSP), your gateway to the fascinating world of pharmacognosy and natural product research in Nigeria. NSP has been at the forefront of promoting the study and application of pharmacognosy, the science of medicinal plants and natural products, in the country.

OBJECTIVES OF NSP

This is why we are NSP

1

To make Pharmacognosy more meaningful and useful to the Community

2

To find ways of improving the methods of teaching the subject in order tp highlight its relevance to the practice of Pharmacy in Nigeria

3

To institute and maintain research collaboration in the field of Pharmacognosy

4

To create a forum for regular meetings in the form of conferences, symposia, workshops, excetera and to promote scientific and other publications

5

To create and occasionally review a possible nationnal Pharmacognosy curriculum. Also to encourage occasional reviews of the Pharmacognosy curriculum offered in Nigerian faculties of Pharmacy.

6

To find effective ways of stimulating the interest of the interest of our Governments and liasing with nationally and internationally related organisations or individuals e.g W.H.O, OAU/STRC, ASP etc
